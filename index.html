Atomic & Ionic Size
Across 3d Series: Decreases (Z_eff \(\uparrow\))
Exceptions: Cr to Cu (Almost Same)
Lanthanoid Contraction: Poor shielding of 4f electrons causes 4d and 5d series (Zr/Hf) to have similar sizes!
Oxidation States

Manganese:+2 to +7 (Max)

⭐ High OS is stabilized by Oxygen and Fluorine due to high electronegativity.
Naming Rule Masterclass
  1. Cation First: Name the cation before the anion.
  2. Ligands (Alpha): List ligands in alphabetical order.
  3. Negative Ligands: End in '-o' (e.g., Cl is Chlorido, CN is Cyanido).
  4. Metal Name: If complex is negative, add -ate to metal (Ferrate, Argentate).
Example: [Co(NH3)4Cl2]Cl

Q: Why Transition Metals are good Catalyst?

Q: Why Interstitial Compounds?
A: Small atoms (H, C, N) fit into the gaps of crystal lattice.
